Yinka Kayode & Co is a trusted firm of Estate Surveyors and Valuers registered with the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (ESVARBON). Established with a vision to deliver excellence in the real estate sector, we offer professional services across property valuation, estate agency, property management, project appraisal, and general real estate consultancy throughout the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Our services cover a wide range of asset classes including land, buildings, plant and machinery, motor vehicles, furniture and fittings, vessels, and trading stock. We provide valuation reports for purposes such as mortgage, insurance, rating, compensation, sales and acquisitions, accounting, privatization, and public listing. With a client-focused approach, our experienced team combines deep industry knowledge with integrity and innovation to deliver reliable, result-oriented solutions that support both private and institutional real estate goals.

Whether guiding clients through investments, conducting accurate valuations, or managing complex projects, Yinka Kayode & Co stands as a name synonymous with trust, excellence, and professional reliability in the Nigerian property landscape.
